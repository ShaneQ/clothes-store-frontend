About the club

The Second Closet Club is a shared wardrobe service that helps you enjoy more of the fashion world without cluttering your house.

Our inventory is carefully selected so you can stay fashionable knowing someone is looking after the maintenance, quality, and environmental impact of what you wear. All you have to do is choose, enjoy, and return.

How it Works

Choose the pieces you like and book the pick up date.

Collect 1 or 2 pieces every week accoding to your membership.

Enjoy your new piece up to 10 days.

Book your favorite garments as many times as you want.

Return your pieces and collect new ones.

Don’t worry about washing. We will take care of it!

More than just Dressing Up

Our clothes tell a lot about who we are and how we see the world. Our garments reflect our personalities, lifestyles, and aspirations.

We also live in a world of constant changes. Adapting our wardrobe to new routines and fashion trends often causes a vicious cycle of desire, buy, wear, and discard.

The 2nd Closet Club comes as an option to break that cycle, showing there is a better way to enjoy fashion.
