Getting into slow fashion and the circular economy.

The textile industry has been criticized for its large contribution to pollution and landfill waste.
We often get caught up in trends that stimulate a vicious cycle of desire, buy, wear, and discard.
The 2nd Closet Club comes as an option to break that cycle, showing there is a better, more conscious way to enjoy fashion.
You don’t need to buy everything you want to wear, you can have much more options when you share.

OUR MISSION

Share the best side of fashion.

Our team is committed to creating a beautiful and unique closet, with garments of good quality, cleaned, repaired, and maintained in the most sustainable way possible we can.

We want to guide our clients through a more environment-friendly way to consume fashion exchanging ownership for sharing access.

We look forward to creating a community of people who uses clothes as an instrument of empowerment and self-expression.
