Are you ready to join our club?

STEP ONE

Request your membership

How many garments do you want to try every month? Make an account and request a membership that allows you to book and collect one or two pieces each time.

We will get back to you with payment details so you can activate your account.

STEP TWO

Browse our shop.

Choose the piece you want to wear this week.
Select the day and time you want to collect and book it.
The meeting point for the collection point is Smithfield Square.

STEP THREE

Enjoy your garment.

Mix and match your new piece to spice up your wardrobe.
You can keep it for up to 10 days and wear it as much as you want.
During that time you can book your next piece so you have a new one ready when you return the current one.

STEP FOUR

Return and start over.

You don’t need to worry about washing, that’s on us!
Just drop off your pieces and collect the newly booked ones.
We will make sure every garment receives the best care and is ready for when you want to wear it again.
