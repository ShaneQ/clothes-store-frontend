Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. Where is the pick up address?

    During your booking you can select one of two options:

    Smithfield - The meeting point is on Smithfield Square in front of the Lighthouse Cinema. Just let us before hand from what time best suit you between 9am to 7pm and we will do our best to match to your schedule. You can reach us though SMS, email, phone or WhatsApp.

    Bragadeiros - The store address is 1 Frenchman's Ln, Mountjoy, Dublin. Your collection will be available during the store work hours Monday to Friday from 11am to 6pm and on Saturdays from 11am to 4pm.

  • 2. Where and when do I return my pieces?

    You can return your pieces anytime within 7 days in any of the 2 collection points. In case you choose to drop at Smithfield please contact us to set up a meeting time.

  • 3. Can I return and pick up new pieces at the same time?

    Yes! You can return your previous booking on the day and time you booked your collection.

  • 4. Do I have to wash or dry clean my items?

    No need to clean your items on your own. We take care of that for you!

  • 5. What if I accidentally stain or damage the clothes?

    We understand that some wear and tear are inevitable. We do our best to repair the garments without extra charges. However, significant damage, loss of the item, and theft aren't covered. If the item is damaged beyond repair or never returned, you will be charged a fee equivalent to the full retail price of the item.

  • 6. Can I keep the clothes if I really like them?

    Unfortunately, no. But you can book your favorite ones as many times as you want.

  • 7. I missed my return date, what happens?

    After 7 days of your collection date, a late fee of € 1.00 per day per garment is applied. Also, your account is blocked for new bookings until the pieces are returned. After 30 days the garment is considered never returned, and a fee equivalent to the full retail price of the item will be charged.

  • 8. Can I rent a single piece?

    Sorry. We don’t have single rental options.

Any other questions? Contact us!
